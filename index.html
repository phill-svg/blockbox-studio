How to use Dreambox Studio

  1. Pick Shirt, Pants or Tee at the top.
  2. Use the coloured tools on the left: Colours, Patterns, Stickers, Text and Upload.
  3. Design each part with the tabs (Front / Back / Sleeves).
  4. Watch the live preview on the right update as you go.
  5. Press Save PNG for Roblox to download your image.
  6. Upload it on Roblox (we'll show you how).

Everything stays on your own computer — no account, nothing is sent anywhere.

Uploading to Roblox 🚀

Your PNG downloaded! A website can't upload to Roblox for you, so finish on Roblox's own site:

  1. Go to create.roblox.com and sign in.
  2. Open Creator Dashboard → Avatar Items → Classics.
  3. Click Upload, choose Shirt / Pants / T-Shirt, and pick your PNG.
  4. Give it a name, then upload. (Selling needs Roblox Premium; a small fee applies.)
  5. Wait for it to pass moderation (usually within a day).

Test for free first: in Roblox Studio, put a Shirt on a Block-rig dummy and set its ShirtTemplate to your image — check it looks right before you pay to upload.

Only upload art you're allowed to use. Copyrighted logos/characters get rejected.
